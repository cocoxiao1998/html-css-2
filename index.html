Fantasy

“Percy Jackson & the Olympians”

Status: finished

One of the first series I have read.

Pros:

  • world building
  • character growth
  • relationship development between the characters

Cons:

  • childish main antagonist
  • inconsistent level of power for the main character

“The Beginning After The End”

Status: currently on Book 5

Started reading this after I came across the webtoon.

Pros:

  • the emotions... I cried
  • timeline
  • characters

Cons:

  • pacing in some arcs
  • overpowered main character
  • school arc was too chaotic

“Awaken Online“

: Currently on book 2.5

Recommended greatly by Justin

Cons:

  • overpowered main character
  • childish antagonist
  • one dimensional side characters
